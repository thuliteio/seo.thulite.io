FAQs

What does Thulite SEO actually do?Thulite SEO handles the technical essentials for you—like meta tags, structured data, sitemaps, and favicons—so your site is optimized for search engines out of the box.
Do I need SEO knowledge to use it?No. Thulite SEO is designed to work with sensible defaults, so you get strong SEO fundamentals without needing deep expertise.
How is this different from traditional SEO tools?Unlike standalone SEO tools, Thulite SEO is built directly into your site. It focuses on technical SEO and automation rather than audits, dashboards, or keyword tracking.
What parts of SEO are covered?It primarily covers technical and on-page SEO fundamentals—like metadata, structured data, and site configuration—so search engines can properly understand and index your content.
Can I still customize SEO settings?Yes. While defaults work well out of the box, you can override and customize settings when you need more control.
Does this improve my rankings automatically?It gives your site a solid technical foundation, which is essential for rankings—but content and strategy still matter.
